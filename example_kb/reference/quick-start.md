# Quick Start

If you just joined the lab, do these things in order:

1. Get your Slack account set up — ask Sara
2. Read the safety protocol (`protocols/safety.md`) before entering the wet lab
3. Read the data handling protocol (`protocols/data-handling.md`)
4. Clone this knowledge base and run `mdvs init` to explore it
5. Introduce yourself in #general
6. Shadow someone for your first week — don't touch equipment unsupervised

For equipment training, coordinate with Sara. She maintains the schedule and will pair you with whoever is most available. REMO can also walk you through basic spectrometer operations, though his explanations tend to be... thorough.
