# Centrifuge Operating Protocol

## Pre-Use Checks

1. Inspect the rotor for cracks or corrosion. Do not use if damaged.
2. Verify that the lid seal is clean and intact.
3. Balance all tubes before loading. Unbalanced loads above 3000 RPM will trigger the automatic shutdown and Sara will know.

## Operation

- Maximum speed: 15000 RPM.
- Maximum run time: 60 minutes per session.
- Temperature range: 4°C to 40°C. For refrigerated runs, allow 10 minutes for pre-cooling.
- Never open the lid while the rotor is spinning. The interlock exists for a reason.

## Sample Requirements

- Use rated tubes only. Standard Eppendorf 1.5mL or 15mL Falcon tubes.
- Seal all tubes. Leaks at high speed create aerosols and ruin the rotor chamber.
- Biohazard samples require the sealed rotor (shelf 2, labeled "BIO"). Log usage in the biohazard register.

## Cleanup

Wipe the rotor and chamber with 70% ethanol after each use. Report any unusual vibration or noise to Sara immediately.
