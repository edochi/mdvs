# Laboratory Safety Protocol

## General Rules

- Lab coats and safety glasses are mandatory in the wet lab at all times.
- No food or drink near equipment. This includes REMO's charging station — it is not a countertop.
- Report all spills immediately. Gold-titanium substrates are expensive and the cleanup procedure depends on the solvent.
- Laser safety training is required before operating the spectrometer. See Sara for scheduling.

## Emergency Procedures

- Fire extinguisher locations: entrance wall, back corridor, near the fume hood.
- Eye wash stations: one in the wet lab, one near the spectrometer room.
- Emergency contact: Sara Dell'Acqua (internal ext. 4401) or campus security (ext. 1000).

## Chemical Handling

All chemicals must be logged in the inventory system before use. MSDS sheets are in the binder next to the fume hood and also available digitally on the shared drive.
